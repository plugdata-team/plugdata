[ikeda~] is an ikeda map chaotic generator, it had two audio outputs generated by the difference equations;
y1[n] = 1 + u * (y1[n-1] * cos(t) - y2[n-1] * sin(t));
y2[n] = u * (y1[n-1] * sin(t) + y2[n-1] * cos(t));
